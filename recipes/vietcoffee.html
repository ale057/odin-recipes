Vietnamese Coffee

Vietnamese coffee is a strong, bold coffee that is served with condensed milk and ice. This drink is perfect for staying energized throughout the day. It's not reccommended to drink past noon if you want to sleep tonight.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of coarsely ground Vietnamese coffee
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • 2 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the sweetened condensed milk in the bottom of a glass.
  2. Set up a Vietnamese coffee filter (phin) on top of the glass.
  3. Add the coarsely ground coffee to the filter and gently shake it to level the grounds.
  4. Pour hot water over the coffee grounds, filling the filter. Allow the coffee to drip slowly into the glass.
  5. Once the coffee has finished dripping, stir the coffee and condensed milk together until well combined.
  6. If desired, add ice cubes to the glass for a refreshing iced Vietnamese coffee.
  7. Enjoy the coffee!
